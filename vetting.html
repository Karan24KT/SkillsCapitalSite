Vetting Process

We have 10,000+ Developer Pool in Inidan IT markets which goes with a continuos vetting.

Skills Capital identifies top development talents and connets them with brilliant IT companies.

Since 2022, Skills Caital has been actively scanning the outsourcing market to identify reputable development agencies with in-demand talent, strong recruitment and retention processes. After handpicking these agencies, Skills Capital invites them to undergo a vetting process before joining its network.

Only 25% of agencies pass SkillsCapital vetting. On average we add 1 agency to the network per week.

SkillsCapital vets the identified agencies.

Agencies are only accepted into the SkillsCapital network if they pass an extensive due diligence process. This process ensures that SkillsCapital can trust the quality of the agency's talent on a high level, based on the strength of its recruitment and retention processes.

100+ agencies in the SkillsCapital network are some of the strongest local employers with a combined talent pool of 10,000+ engineers.

Agencies in the SkillsCapital network vet their talent as they hire and retain it.

All of SkillsCapital’s engineers pass a multi-stage screening process which starts when they get hired by the agencies in the SkillsCapital network, continues as they work for those agencies and get re-vetted by SkillsCapital over time.

Hundreds of potentially suitable candidates get reviewed by SkillsCapital for each open position. Top matches undergo in-depth vetting.

SkillsCapital re-vets talent every time it runs the Matching Process.

The second screening stage is conducted by SkillsCapital during a tailored Matching Process for every new customer role, based on the Ideal Candidate Profile created by the customer and our Matching Expert.

2-3 candidates are sent to you for review. On average SkillsCapital's customers interview just 1.75 to close a role.
