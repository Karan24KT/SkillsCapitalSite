Client Shares Requirement with us


There are Multiple Options for Doing That:

1. Shares Requirements Directly on the AI Platform
2. Discusses Requirements with their Dedicated Account Manager
3. Sends Email or Voice note with the Requirement Details

The AI Platform Captures & Analyzes the Requirements


1. The Platform / Operations Team Reviews, Validates The Requirements.
2. The AI / NLP Engine Analyzes the Requirements and Fill Any Missing Gaps.
3. The Platform Marks the Requirement as 'Ready to Process'.

The AI / ML Based Matching Engine Shortlists Most Suitable Profiles


1. The Matching Profiles are Shared with the Client on their Dashboard
2. The Dedicated Account Manager also updates Client on Shortlisted Profiles
3. The Profile Includes the Candidates Summary Along with Vetting Details

The Client Selects the Final Tech Experts / Teams


1. The Client Shortlists Profiles for Further Interview & Assessment
2. Client's Dedicated Account Manager Helps Coordinate the Interviews
3. AI Platform does Final Vetting and Reference Checks For Selected Candidates
